BidDeliv (Live Auction Arena)

This is the heart of the platform where real battles unfold for your cargo. Watch as verified logistics companies — each represented by a unique animal avatar — compete in an exciting real-time race to win your shipment. The countdown timer tracks the auction progress, while animated creatures dash across the screen with each new bid, creating a visual spectacle of genuine competition. Hit "Accept" anytime during the 12-hour auction window when you're satisfied with the price, or sit back and enjoy watching carriers fight for your business.

Container Search Map

An interactive world map showing real-time container availability and cargo locations across major shipping routes. Users can track their shipments visually, identify container positions in key logistics hubs, and monitor the China-USA corridor traffic that represents AiDeliv's primary service area.

Holidays Calendar

A planning tool displaying upcoming non-business days in origin and destination countries. Shows holidays like "The holiday of the dead" (3 days) and Chinese New Year (7 days) with exact dates, helping clients anticipate potential shipping delays and plan logistics around peak closure periods.

Real-time analytics showing CBM freight pricing trends from the Shanghai Containerized Freight Index. Triple-Layer Protection Standard

Maximum Security Without Premium Pricing


Every shipment includes three layers of protection at no extra cost:


Layer 1 — Automatic Insurance: Coverage up to invoice value (max $100,000) included standard. Lost, damaged, or stolen cargo is covered without buying additional insurance that others charge 1-3% extra for.


Layer 2 — Consolidated Volume Leverage: When carriers handle your shipment, they're protecting their relationship with our entire network. One mistake with your cargo could cost them access to hundreds of monthly shipments. This fear drives exceptional service.


Layer 3 — Platform Guarantee: AiDeliv stands between you and problems. We handle all carrier disputes, insurance claims, and issue resolution. You're never alone fighting for your rights — our team fights for you. Compare this to shipping alone: fighting damage claims for months, chasing insurance that finds excuses to deny coverage, dealing with carriers who barely remember your name. With AiDeliv, protection isn't optional — it's  fundamental.
